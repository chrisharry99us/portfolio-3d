About

I started my career as a frontend developer — building user interfaces, learning how design translates into code, and developing a strong foundation in how software is experienced from the user's perspective.

From there I pivoted fully into software engineering, spending several years in backend and full-stack roles building production systems — microservices, REST APIs, Kubernetes deployments — and developing a deep understanding of how technology works at scale.

I've since moved into data analytics and machine learning in business settings, applying both engineering and analytical thinking to real-world problems. I now work at the intersection of data science, AI, and business intelligence — building systems that turn complex data into decisions.
