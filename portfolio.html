About

I started my career as a Frontend developer — building user interfaces, learning how design translates into code, and developing a strong foundation in how software is experienced from the user's perspective.

I've since moved into data analytics and machine learning in business settings, applying both engineering and analytical thinking to real-world problems.

I now work at the intersection of Data Science, AI, and business intelligence — building systems that turn complex data into decisions.
